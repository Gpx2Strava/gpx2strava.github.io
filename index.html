GPX2Strava

Create custom running routes and export as GPX files

Draw a route on the map to get started

How to Use

  1. Click "Draw Route" button to activate drawing mode
  2. Click on the map to create waypoints for your route
  3. Continue clicking to add more points to your route
  4. Double-click on the last point to finish drawing
  5. Click "Export GPX" to download your route file
  6. Upload the GPX file to Strava or your preferred fitness app

Tip: You can also use the drawing toolbar that appears on the map (the line icon) to start drawing.
