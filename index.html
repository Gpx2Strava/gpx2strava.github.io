GPX2Strava

Create custom running routes and export as GPX files

Draw a route on the map to get started

How to Use

  1. Click "Draw Route" to start drawing
  2. Click on the map to create waypoints for your route
  3. Double-click to finish drawing
  4. Click "Export GPX" to download your route file
  5. Upload the GPX file to Strava or your preferred fitness app
